What is DBVMS ?

Not only limited to Mechatronics students, DBVMS Mechatronic Society is a newly established student initiative which promotes and provides pathways to engage in the DBVMS community. We are a student based society aimed at bringing together students for networking, discussions as well as gather resources and hold events to assist students throughout their program and future career.
